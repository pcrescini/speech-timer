Speech Timer

I joined Toastmasters International in July 2015 and have given countless speeches at my home club, Talking Bull Toastmasters.

One of the skills we practice in Toastmasters is speaking within our allocated time period. During speeches, a designated timer would keep track of the time and provide visual clues to the speaker once

When the pandemic forced Toastmasters clubs to meet virtually, having an easy to use and readily accessible digital timing tool for speeches was paramount.

Inspired by the Toastmasters Speech Timer created by Caleb Grove, I wanted to see if I could recreate this tool from scratch and customize it to my personal needs.
